# Product Images

Please add your actual precast product photos to this directory with the following filenames:

1. **precast-pavers.jpg** - Photo of your precast paving stones/pavers
2. **u-drains.jpg** - Photo of your U-shaped drainage channels
3. **box-culverts.jpg** - Photo of your box culvert products
4. **retaining-walls.jpg** - Photo of your precast retaining wall systems

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Recommended size**: 800px wide minimum (1200px-1600px ideal)
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 or 4:3 works best
- **Quality**: High-resolution photos showing the actual products in use or display

## Tips for Best Results

- Use well-lit, professional photos
- Show the products installed/in use if possible
- Ensure images are clear and showcase the quality of your products
- Consider using photos from actual project installations
